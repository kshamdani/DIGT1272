Why Accessibility Matters

In today's digital society, web accessibility is not a luxury—it's a fundamental right. As more governments, education systems, and essential services move online, people with disabilities face real barriers to participation.

Inclusive design strengthens digital communities for everyone.

Key Statistics

  • 1 in 5 Canadians have a disability
  • WCAG 2.1 is the global accessibility standard
  • 100% of digital content can be accessible
